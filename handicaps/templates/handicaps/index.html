Tour Standard - Just how good are they?

{% endblock header %} {% block content %}

PGA Tour players are the best golfers in the world. They consistently post excellent scores on the hardest courses in the world.

Tour Standard calculates just how good they are by using the Australian Calculations for handicaps to guage just how good they are.

In order to calculate their handicaps Tour Standard factors in course difficulty and setup difficulty. From this adjusted score, the top 8 scores of their most recent 20 games calculate their playing handicap.
